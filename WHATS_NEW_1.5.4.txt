Individual Note Highlights

Each note now gets its own separate highlight background instead of one large 
block covering everything. White box and black box highlight styles now wrap 
each note individually with proper spacing and rounded corners.

What's improved:
- Highlight backgrounds render per-note (no more giant single block)
- White and black box styles have better padding and rounded corners
- Shadow effects apply cleanly to individual note boxes
- General polish and stability improvements

Thank you for using NoteWall.
